### How to Get to Sejong University

The official Sejong University address is 209 Neungdong-ro, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul. For route planning, you can use <a target='_blank' alt='link' rel='noopener noreferrer' href='https://goo.gl/maps/Qmju9ksw6Qd6UGiM7'>Google Map directions</a>, <a target='_blank' alt='link' rel='noopener noreferrer' href='https://map.kakao.com/?map_type=TYPE_MAP&target=car&rt=%2C%2C516480%2C1124796&rt1=&rt2=%EC%84%B8%EC%A2%85%EB%8C%80%ED%95%99%EA%B5%90&rtIds=%2C7949668&rtTypes=%2CPLACE'>Kakao Map directions</a>, or the <a target='_blank' alt='link' rel='noopener noreferrer' href='https://en.sejong.ac.kr/eng/Direction.do'>official Sejong direction page</a>.

#### From the Airport

<ul>
	<li>Airport Limousine Bus `6013` connects Incheon Airport (ICN) directly to the Sejong University area.</li>
	<li>Fare: approximately `17,000 KRW` for adults.</li>
	<li>Boarding at Incheon Airport.</li>
	<li>Get off at `Sejong University (어린이대공원역 / Children's Grand Park Station)` near the Sejong University main gate.</li>
	<li>Operating hours are generally `05:45-23:05`, but it is best to confirm the latest timetable for your terminal before travel.</li>
	<li>Or take the AREX Commuter train from the airport and transfer at Gimpo Airport Station.</li>
	<li>From Gimpo Airport Station, take Subway Line 5 to Gunja Station.</li>
	<li>At Gunja Station, transfer to Line 7.</li>
	<li>Get off at Children's Grand Park (Sejong Univ.) Station. Sejong University is near Exit 6.</li>
</ul>

#### By Subway
