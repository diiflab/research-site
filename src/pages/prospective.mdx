# Guidelines for Prospective Students

:::tip **Important Notice (Please Read First)**

AINTLab does **not** offer research assistantships (RA) or fully funded positions.
Emails requesting scholarships or funding support will **not receive a reply**, **unless explicitly stated in a separate official announcement**.
Prospective students are expected to be **self-funded or supported by external scholarships**.
:::

These guidelines are intended for **self-funded prospective applicants** (PhD, MSc, or Internship) who wish to contact the Director of AINTLab. Due to the high volume of inquiries, **only well-prepared, relevant, and personalized emails are considered**. Generic emails and funding requests are routinely ignored.

## Purpose of Your Email
Your email should have a **clear academic purpose**, such as:
- Expressing interest in joining AINTLab (PhD, MSc, or Internship)
- Asking about research alignment or supervision availability
- Seeking guidance on the formal application process

## Final Notes
- A polite, well-prepared email increases the chance of a response.
- Not receiving a reply usually means the email did not meet the above criteria.
- Always follow **official lab announcements** for funding or position openings.

Be respectful, be specific, and be prepared.
